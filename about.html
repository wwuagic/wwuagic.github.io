We're the WWU Association for Gender Inclusion in Computing.

Our mission is to support gender-related diversity in computer science. We strive to build an empowering environment for all people who are a minority in computer science due to their gender and/or gender presentation (women, non-binary people, intersex people, gender non-conforming people, etc.) via opportunities for community, professional development, and leadership.

Our activities include

  • Weekly Club Meetings
  • Company Tours
  • Game Nights
  • Tech Talks
  • Professional Development Panels
  • Hackathons
